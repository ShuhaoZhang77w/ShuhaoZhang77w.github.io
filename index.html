About Me

Hi, I am Shuhao Zhang (张书豪). Welcome to my personal page!

Currently, I am a Master's candidate (expected to graduate in June 2025) in Communication Engineering at Southeast University, Nanjing, China, within the School of Information Science and Engineering, under the supervisions of Prof. Wei Kang ( Google Scholar ) and Prof. Nan Liu ( Google Scholar ) .

Before this, I received my B.Eng. degree in Electronic Information Engineering from Xidian University, Xi'an, China in 2022, within the School of Electronic Engineering.

Generally, my research interests include

  • Machine learning
  • Information theory
  • Communications
  • Network coding
... and their intersections.

Currently, I am specifically focusing on

  • Data-driven and neural methods for the estimation and optimization of the fundamental limits in information theoretical problems
  • Applications of neural networks in communication/sensing/ISAC systems

Through my past research, I am familiar with topics including

  • Neural estimation and optimization of channel capacity/rate-distortion function/multi-user channel capacity
  • Entropy region / Linear rank / Matroid / LP bound and polyhedron algorithm, and their applications to network coding problems
  • Coded caching

I also know something about

  • Network information theory
  • Finite blocklength regime

Besides, I am eager and ready to explore other related fields as well.
