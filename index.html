About Me

Hi, I am Shuhao Zhang (张书豪). Welcome to my personal page!

Currently, I am a Master's student (expected to graduate in June 2025) in Communication Engineering at Southeast University, Nanjing, China, within the School of Information Science and Engineering, under the supervision of Prof. Wei Kang ( Google Scholar ) and Prof. Nan Liu ( Google Scholar , Faculty Profile ) .

Before this, I received my Bachelor's degree in Electronic Information Engineering from Xidian University, Xi'an, China in 2022, within the School of Electronic Engineering.

Generally, my research interests include

  • Information theory
  • Machine learning
  • Communications
  • Network coding
and their intersections.

Currently, my research focuses on machine learning methods for information-theoretic problems. More specifically, I am exploring

  • Data-driven estimation and optimization for fundamental limits in information-theoretic problems
  • Applications of machine learning in communication, sensing, ISAC systems

Through my past research, I have gained experience in topics including

  • Neural estimation and optimization of channel capacity/rate-distortion function/multi-user channel capacity
  • Entropy region, linear rank, matroid, LP bound and polyhedron algorithm, along with their applications to network coding problems
  • Coded caching

I am also familiar with fundamental techniques in

  • Network information theory
  • Finite blocklength regime

Besides, I am eager to explore other related fields as well.
