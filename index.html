About Me

Hi, I am Shuhao Zhang (张书豪).

Currently, I am a Master candidate in Communication Engineering at Southeast University, Nanjing, China, within the School of Information Science and Engineering, under the supervisions of Prof. Wei Kang ( Google Scholar ) and Prof. Nan Liu ( Google Scholar ) .

Before this, I received my B.Eng. degree in Electronic Information Engineering from Xidian University, Xi'an, China in 2022, with in the School of Electronic Engineering.

My research interests include information theory, network coding, machine learning, communications, ......, their intersections and related directions. Currently, I am specifically familiar with entropy region, coded caching, and neural estimation and optimization in information theory.
